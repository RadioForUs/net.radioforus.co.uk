The Youth Voice on The Airwaves.

A dedicated space for young amateur radio operators to connect, chat, and build confidence on the air.

The Mission

Started by M9JAK & 2E0KOF to break the ice for young operators and encourage consistent activity on Digital Voice networks.

The Success

Our trial net saw 20 check-ins from across the network! We are now working to make this a regular fixture in your radio calendar.

How to Join

The NetForUs Youth Net is hosted on the FreeSTAR Network. You can join via AllStar, EchoLink, DMR, or P25 hotspots.
